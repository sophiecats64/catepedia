Where to get a cat

There are many ways to welcome a feline friend into your home, from shelters, starys, cat sellers, even to families which cat has given birth.

However, the 'better' way to acquire a cat, is by adopting them. You can go about doing this by looking at different shelters and seeing which cat is best for you.

in Ireland, there are many shelters, however the biggest and most prominent is the DSPCA.

DSPCA

This is a walk-in shelter in Dublin, that is open from Tuesday to Sunday, 12-4pm.

on the DSPCA website, you can find and look at all the cats that are available to adopt and do your research on them prior to arriving at the shelter.
