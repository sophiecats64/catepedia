Catapedia

Cats are one of the most popular pet type in todays world. They're fluffy, cute and intelligant. how can you not love them?

This website is for all the cat lovers out there, whether you already have a cat, looking to get one or are just interested in learning about cats, this website is for you!

Here you will find funny cat videos, photos and an introduction to my page. Please scroll down and 'aww' at the cuteness!

If you wish to learn more about cats, however, please check out the different webpages on this website! There's information about the different cat breeds, interesting facts, how you would go about adopting a cat (adopt, don't shop!) and random interesting cat facts I think you'll enjoy.

Please also check the form page , and write a comment! I'd love to hear from you all and share in our adoration of the best animal out there :3

About this Website
Is the website colours based on a calico cat? yes! You have a keen eye. I have created this website to use the colours of a calico, orange, brown and yellow! (the blue is for the eyes.) as calicos are one of my favourite breeds.
Why is this website called 'Catapedia'?
simple! I named it after a mix up of the word 'cat' and 'wikipedia', because this is an informational website centered around cats! also, I love puns and combination words
Cats or Dogs?
Cats duh! I love all animals, but I adore cats the most. they're just so fluffy and sweet. (I say this as if it's not obvious from the whole website around cats)
